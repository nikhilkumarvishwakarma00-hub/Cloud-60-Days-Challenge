## Objective
Learn how Chain-of-Thought Prompting can generate a personalized career roadmap.

## Prompt Used

## Biggest Insight

My biggest insight was that becoming an AI Engineer is not just about learning AI models. It also requires deployment skills, portfolio projects, networking, and continuous practical learning.

## Key Learnings

- Chain-of-Thought prompting creates structured and detailed outputs.
- Breaking a long-term goal into phases makes learning easier.
- Projects and consistency are more important than consuming tutorials.
- AI can help create personalized learning roadmaps.
