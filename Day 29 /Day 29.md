# Day 29 - Operation Lifeline

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Supply Chain Crisis Simulator to practice executive decision-making during business disruptions.

---

## Features

- Random Company Profile Generation
- Supply Chain Crisis Simulation
- Crisis Response Selection
- Supplier Negotiation
- CEO Leadership Assessment
- AI Investment Decision
- Executive Dashboard
- Business Scorecard

---

## Workflow

1. Start the simulation.
2. Review the generated company profile.
3. Analyze the supply chain crisis.
4. Select three crisis response actions.
5. Complete supplier negotiations.
6. Answer CEO leadership questions.
7. Select AI investment opportunities.
8. Review the executive dashboard.
9. Analyze business performance.
10. Replay different scenarios.

---

## Crisis Scenarios Tested

### Scenario 1
Raw Material Shortage

### Scenario 2
Supplier Bankruptcy

### Scenario 3
Transportation Delay

### Scenario 4
Global Supply Chain Disruption

---

## Leadership Decisions

- Supplier diversification
- Inventory optimization
- Emergency procurement
- Customer communication
- Risk mitigation planning

---

## AI Investment Choices

- Demand Forecasting AI
- Supply Chain Analytics Platform

---

## Key Learnings

- Supply chains require proactive risk management.
- Leadership decisions directly impact business performance.
- Supplier relationships are critical during crises.
- AI improves forecasting and operational resilience.
- Data-driven decision-making reduces uncertainty.
