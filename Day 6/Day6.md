# Day 6 - Resume Optimization & ATS Analysis

## Objective
Learn how AI can analyze and optimize a resume for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

## Process

1. Uploaded my resume to Claude.
2. Used the Resume Optimizer prompt.
3. Reviewed the ATS analysis.
4. Generated an optimized one-page resume.
5. Compared the original and optimized versions.

## ATS Analysis Summary

### Strengths Identified
- Educational background clearly mentioned.
- Technical skills section included.
- Relevant certifications and projects present.

### Areas for Improvement
- Better keyword optimization for ATS.
- Stronger action verbs in project descriptions.
- Improved formatting and structure.
- More measurable achievements.

## Improvements in Optimized Resume

- ATS-friendly format.
- Better keyword usage.
- Improved readability.
- Stronger project descriptions.
- More professional presentation.

## Key Learnings

- ATS systems rely heavily on keywords.
- Resume structure affects visibility.
- Clear and measurable achievements improve impact.
- AI can significantly improve resume quality.

## Comparison

### Original Resume
- Basic formatting
- Limited ATS optimization
- Generic descriptions

### Optimized Resume
- ATS-friendly layout
- Better keywords
- Improved clarity and impact

## Conclusion

AI-powered resume optimization helps create a more professional and ATS-friendly resume, increasing the chances of passing recruiter screening systems.
