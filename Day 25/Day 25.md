---

## Objective

## Startup Details

### Problem

Customers struggle to discover quality restaurants, compare menus, read trusted reviews, and reserve tables efficiently.

### Solution

A smart restaurant platform that helps users:

- Discover restaurants
- Compare menus
- Book tables
- View verified reviews
- Receive AI-powered food recommendations

### Target Customers

- College Students
- Working Professionals
- Families
- Food Enthusiasts
- Travelers

### Revenue Model

- Restaurant Subscription Plans
- Featured Listings
- Advertisements
- Premium Membership
- Table Reservation Fees
