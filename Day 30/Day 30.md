# Day 30 - Supply Chain Optimizer

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Supply Chain Optimizer to simulate business decisions and analyze their impact on operational performance.

---

## Features

- Beginner-Friendly Introduction
- Random Company Profile Generator
- Supplier Strategy Selection
- Factory Location Planning
- Warehouse Strategy
- Transportation Selection
- Inventory Strategy
- Live Business Metrics
- Supply Chain Score
- Risk Analysis
- AI Recommendations
- Optimization Dashboard

---

## Workflow

1. Started the simulation.
2. Reviewed the generated company profile.
3. Selected a supplier strategy.
4. Chose the factory location.
5. Selected the warehouse strategy.
6. Selected transportation methods.
7. Configured inventory strategy.
8. Observed real-time business metrics.
9. Reviewed the optimization dashboard.
10. Analyzed the final supply chain score.
