## Comparison of Responses

### 1. Without Role Prompt
Prompt:
`Explain Role-Based Prompting.`

Observation:
- Gave a general explanation.
- Limited structure and detail.
- Generic examples.
- Suitable for basic understanding.

---

### 2. Founder Persona
Prompt:
`You are a startup founder. Explain Role-Based Prompting.`

Observation:
- Focused on business value and decision-making.
- Explained how role-based prompting can improve productivity and outcomes.
- More strategic and goal-oriented.
- Emphasized problem-solving and execution.

---

### 3. Developer Persona
Prompt:
`You are a senior software developer. Explain Role-Based Prompting.`

Observation:
- Focused on technical implementation.
- Explained how prompts can improve coding assistance and debugging.
- Included practical development use cases.
- More detailed from an engineering perspective.

---

## Key Learning

The same question produced different responses depending on the assigned role.

- Without Role → Generic answer
- Founder Persona → Business-focused answer
- Developer Persona → Technical answer

Role-Based Prompting helps generate more relevant, targeted, and useful responses based on the perspective required.
