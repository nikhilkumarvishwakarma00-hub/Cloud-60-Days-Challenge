# Day 37 - Task Compass

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Task Compass application to understand task ownership, task routing, and collaboration across different workplace environments.

---

## Features

- Workplace Type Selection
- Task Ownership Challenge
- Task Routing Simulation
- Collaboration Challenge
- Organizational Thinking Dashboard
- Performance Summary
- Replay with Different Workplace Types

---

## Workflow

1. Selected a workplace type.
2. Generated the complete HTML application.
3. Opened the simulator locally.
4. Completed Stage 1: Who Owns This?
5. Completed Stage 2: Task Routing.
6. Completed Stage 3: Collaboration Challenge.
7. Reviewed the Organizational Thinking Dashboard.
8. Replayed using another workplace type.

---

## Stages Completed

### Stage 1 – Who Owns This?

Identified the correct owner for different workplace tasks and responsibilities.

### Stage 2 – Task Routing

Assigned tasks to the appropriate department or team member to improve workflow efficiency.

### Stage 3 – Collaboration Challenge

Worked through cross-functional scenarios requiring teamwork and communication.

---

## Dashboard Metrics

- Task Ownership Accuracy
- Routing Efficiency
- Collaboration Score
- Organizational Thinking Score
- Overall Performance

---

## Key Learnings

- Clear ownership prevents confusion.
- Proper task routing improves efficiency.
- Cross-functional collaboration strengthens productivity.
- Organizational thinking helps solve workplace challenges.
- AI can simulate real business workflows for learning.
