# Day 28 - Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Hospital Admission Readiness Simulator to evaluate patient readiness before hospital admission.

---

## Features

- Provider Information
- Physician Details
- Diagnosis Selection
- Admission Type
- Prior Authorization Status
- Admission Date Configuration
- Admission Readiness Score
- Documentation Review
- Insurance Validation
- Bed Availability Check
- Clinical Risk Assessment
- Governance Snapshot
- Final Admission Decision

---

## Workflow

1. Enter provider details.
2. Select physician information.
3. Choose diagnosis.
4. Select admission type.
5. Configure Prior Authorization status.
6. Review initial readiness score.
7. Complete required workflow actions.
8. Resolve pending authorization or appeals.
9. Reduce documentation and insurance risks.
10. Review Governance Snapshot.
11. Analyze the final admission decision.

---

## Diagnosis Scenarios Tested

### Scenario 1
Elective Surgery

### Scenario 2
Emergency Admission

### Scenario 3
Medical Observation

### Scenario 4
Inpatient Admission

---

## Key Learnings

- Hospital admission involves multiple validation steps.
- Prior Authorization significantly impacts admission readiness.
- Complete documentation reduces approval delays.
- Insurance verification is essential before admission.
- AI can simplify healthcare workflow visualization.
