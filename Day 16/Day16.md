# Day 16 - Custom Claude Skill: Stock Fundamental Research

## Objective

Create a reusable Claude Custom Skill for stock fundamental analysis and use it to generate professional research reports.

---

## Skill Details

### Skill Name
stock-fundamental-research

### Description
Analyze Indian and global listed companies using fundamentals, financial statements, business quality, competitive advantages, valuation, risks, and growth prospects. Generate evidence-based research reports and investor-friendly summaries. Never provide direct buy, sell, or hold recommendations.

## Key Observations

### TCS

Strengths:
- Strong brand reputation
- Consistent profitability
- Strong cash flow generation

Watch Points:
- Slower growth compared to emerging IT segments
- Dependence on global IT spending

### Infosys

Strengths:
- Strong digital transformation business
- Consistent revenue growth
- Healthy return ratios

Watch Points:
- Competitive pressure in IT services
- Global economic slowdown risks
