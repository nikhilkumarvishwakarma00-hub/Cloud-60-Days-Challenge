# Day 36 - Cognitive Pattern Explorer

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Cognitive Pattern Explorer that helps users understand their thinking patterns through interactive scenarios, drag-and-drop activities, and reflection exercises.

---

## Features

- Calm Mode
- Stress Mode
- Interactive Story Scenarios
- Drag-and-Drop Activities
- Thinking Sequence Challenge
- Personalized Reflection Journal
- Thinking Pattern Comparison
- Replay Experience

---

## Workflow

1. Started the simulator.
2. Selected Calm Mode / Stress Mode.
3. Completed Chapter 1 scenario-based questions.
4. Finished Chapter 2 drag-and-drop activities.
5. Completed Chapter 3 thinking sequence challenge.
6. Reviewed the Reflection Journal.
7. Compared thinking tendencies across chapters.
8. Replayed the experience using the opposite mode.

## Reflection Report

The simulator generated a personalized reflection journal highlighting:

- Decision-making style
- Emotional awareness
- Problem-solving approach
- Thinking consistency
- Areas for improvement

---

## Key Learnings

- Different situations influence our thinking patterns.
- Stress can change decision-making behavior.
- Reflection improves self-awareness.
- Interactive learning increases engagement.
- AI can support cognitive skill development.
