# Day 31 - AI Supply Chain Control Tower

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Supply Chain Control Tower to monitor operational alerts, prioritize incidents, and improve supply chain performance through interactive decision-making.

---

## Features

- Real-Time Operational Alerts
- Incident Prioritization
- Corrective Action Selection
- Live KPI Dashboard
- Multiple Alert Management
- Operational Performance Tracking
- Supply Chain Health Monitoring
- Final Performance Dashboard
- Replay Simulation

---

## Workflow

1. Read simulator instructions.
2. Monitored incoming operational alerts.
3. Prioritized incidents based on urgency.
4. Selected corrective actions.
5. Observed KPI changes after each decision.
6. Managed multiple incidents simultaneously.
7. Completed the simulation.
8. Reviewed the operational dashboard.
9. Replayed the simulation with different strategies.

---

## Operational Alerts Tested

- Supplier Delay
- Warehouse Capacity Issue
- Transportation Disruption
- Inventory Shortage
- Demand Spike
- Production Delay

---

## KPIs Monitored

- On-Time Delivery
- Inventory Level
- Order Fulfillment
- Customer Satisfaction
- Operational Cost
- Supply Chain Efficiency
- Risk Score

---

## Key Learnings

- Real-time monitoring improves operational visibility.
- Quick decision-making minimizes business disruptions.
- KPI tracking helps measure operational performance.
- AI enhances supply chain management through predictive insights.
- Effective prioritization reduces operational risk.
