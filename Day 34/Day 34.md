# Day 34 - Marketing Detective

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Marketing Detective Simulator to investigate marketing campaigns, analyze evidence, and identify the primary reasons behind campaign success or failure.

---

## Features

- Theme Selection
- Marketing Investigation Board
- Drag-and-Drop Evidence Cards
- Campaign Evidence Analysis
- Root Cause Identification
- AI Expert Verdict
- Marketing Learning Report
- Randomized Marketing Cases

---

## Workflow

1. Started the simulator.
2. Selected a preferred color theme.
3. Accepted the first marketing investigation.
4. Reviewed campaign evidence.
5. Dragged and organized evidence cards.
6. Identified the primary marketing mistake.
7. Reviewed the AI expert explanation.
8. Studied the Marketing Learning Report.
9. Replayed different investigation scenarios.

---

## Investigation Cases

### Case 1
Poor Audience Targeting

### Case 2
Weak Marketing Message

### Case 3
Low Conversion Rate

### Case 4
Ineffective Social Media Campaign

---

## Evidence Reviewed

- Campaign Metrics
- Audience Data
- Advertisement Performance
- Conversion Reports
- Customer Feedback
- Engagement Analytics

---

## Final Verdict

The simulator identified the primary marketing issue and provided practical recommendations to improve campaign performance.

---

## Key Learnings

- Marketing decisions should be based on data.
- Audience research is critical.
- Campaign metrics reveal hidden problems.
- AI can simplify marketing analysis.
- Case-based learning improves marketing skills.
