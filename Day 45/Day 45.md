# Day 45 - AI Decision Strategist

## Objective

Build an AI-powered Decision Strategist that guides users through difficult decisions using structured questions, objective analysis, and interactive reports.

---

## Features

- Guided Decision Interview
- Option Comparison
- Assumption Buster
- Cognitive Bias Detection
- Decision Matrix
- Premortem Analysis
- 7-Day Test Plan
- Final Verdict
- Shareable Decision Cards

---

## Workflow

1. Started the application.
2. Answered the four decision interview questions.
3. Generated the Decision Report.
4. Compared available options.
5. Reviewed assumptions and cognitive biases.
6. Analyzed the Decision Matrix.
7. Studied the Premortem Analysis.
8. Reviewed the 7-Day Test Plan.
9. Read the Final Verdict.
10. Captured screenshots.

---

## Decision Framework

### Step 1

Identify the actual decision and available options.

### Step 2

Understand goals, priorities, and timeline.

### Step 3

Analyze intuition and hidden obstacles.

### Step 4

Evaluate risks, reversibility, and long-term impact.

## Key Learnings

- Breaking complex decisions into smaller questions improves clarity.
- Cognitive biases can influence decision-making.
- A structured decision matrix helps compare options objectively.
- Premortem analysis highlights risks before taking action.
- AI can support thoughtful decision-making without replacing human judgment.
