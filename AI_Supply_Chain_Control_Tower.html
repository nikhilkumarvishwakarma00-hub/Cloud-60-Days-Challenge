📋 How to Play

You are the Head of Operations. Alerts stream in from your global supply chain — decide fast.

Your Goal

Maximize your overall score by resolving alerts with the best available action before the 3-minute timer ends.

Priorities

  • 🔴 Critical — Resolve immediately. Ignoring causes heavy KPI damage.
  • 🟠 Medium — Address within the time window shown.
  • 🔵 Low — Can wait but will worsen if ignored too long.

Actions

  • Green buttons = Best outcome for the situation.
  • Blue buttons = Decent outcome, minor cost trade-off.
  • Orange buttons = Risky — may help short-term, hurt long-term.
  • Red buttons = Likely to make things worse.

KPIs

  • Service Level, Customer Satisfaction, Inventory Health, Transportation Efficiency — higher is better.
  • Operating Cost — lower is better.
  • Revenue Protected — keep it high by resolving critical alerts fast.

Tips

  • Unresolved alerts auto-expire and apply their worst-case penalty.
  • Some actions have delayed consequences — keep watching your KPIs.
  • Use Pause when you need to think.
