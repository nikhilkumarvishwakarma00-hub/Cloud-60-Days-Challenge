# Day 18 – Brain Dump Action Planner Skill

## Objective
Create and test a reusable Claude Custom Skill that converts messy notes, meeting transcripts, brainstorming sessions, and voice memos into structured action plans and interactive dashboards.

---

## Skill Information

### Skill Name
brain-dump-action-planner

### Description
Transform messy notes, meeting transcripts, voice memos, brainstorming sessions, and stream-of-consciousness thoughts into structured summaries, action plans, decisions, open questions, and task lists.
