# Day 27 - Prior Authorization Story Simulator

## Objective

Build an interactive story-based simulator to explain the Prior Authorization process using AI-generated HTML.

---

## Story Overview

The simulator follows Rahul's healthcare journey while Priya explains every step of the Prior Authorization process.

---

## Features

- Interactive Storytelling
- 8 Story Chapters
- Branching Choices
- Progress Bar
- Educational Dialogues
- Multiple Decision Paths
- Workflow Summary
- Restart Story Option

---

## Story Chapters

### Chapter 1
Patient visits the doctor.

### Chapter 2
Treatment recommendation.

### Chapter 3
Prior Authorization required.

### Chapter 4
Provider submits documents.

### Chapter 5
Insurance company reviews the request.

### Chapter 6
Additional information requested.

### Chapter 7
Final approval decision.

### Chapter 8
Treatment begins and key takeaways.

---

## Choices Explored

- Submit complete documents
- Submit incomplete documents
- Wait for review
- Respond to additional requests
- Appeal when required

---

## Key Learnings

- Prior Authorization is a multi-step approval process.
- Missing documentation delays approvals.
- Communication between patient, provider, and payer is essential.
- Interactive storytelling improves understanding of complex workflows.
- AI can generate engaging educational applications.
